# Making Wolfram Tech Available As A Foundation Tool For LLM Systems

**The quick take**
Wolfram as a foundation tool gives your LLM stack a math and logic engine it can trust.

**What changed and why it matters**
- The update tightens connections between language models and symbolic computation.
- You can route hard math and data-heavy reasoning to deterministic tools.
- This reduces hallucinated calculations in high-stakes outputs.

**How to use this this week**

**Proof points worth tracking**
