What It Can't Do

Even if "Kling 3.0," "Sora 2 Pro," and "Seedance 1.5 Pro" were real and powerful, AI video tools generally have limits:

  • Understand Complex Stories: They struggle with long, detailed plots. Making a full movie with many characters and deep emotions is hard for AI.
  • Perfect Realism Every Time: While often impressive, AI-generated videos can sometimes look "off." They might have strange movements or objects that don't quite make sense.
  • Handle Nuance and Emotion: Capturing subtle human expressions or true artistic vision is difficult. AI creates based on what it learned, not lived experience.
  • Instant Real-time Interaction: Creating a video on the fly that reacts perfectly to live input is still a challenge. There's often a processing time.
  • Accurate Historical or Scientific Detail: Without careful guidance, the AI might make mistakes in facts or accuracy. It might create fictional details.
