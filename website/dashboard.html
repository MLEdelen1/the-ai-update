How to read these numbers

If visits rise but subscribers stay flat, tighten your lead magnet CTA and simplify forms. If offer clicks are high but conversions are low, revise the sponsor package copy or reduce friction in booking steps.

A practical daily workflow is: check top metric changes, review latest subscribers, then make one focused content or offer adjustment. Avoid changing multiple variables in one day.
